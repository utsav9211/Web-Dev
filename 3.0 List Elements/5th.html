utsav's Recipes For The Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredents

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2(1/4) teaspon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yuk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the fillig :

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1(1/2)tablespon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup buttter

Instuctions

  1. mix the milk with yeast ,sugar ,eggs
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined into dough .
  4. knead the dough for 10 min
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap .leave
    it somewhere to rise for 2 hour
